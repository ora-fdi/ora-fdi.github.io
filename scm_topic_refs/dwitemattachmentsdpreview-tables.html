DW_ITEM_ATTACHMENTS_D (Preview)

This table requires activation of Item Management functional area. This table stores attachment details for both Item & Item Revision Level. If ENTITY_NAME is 'ITEM_ENTITY' then Item Attachments ,ENTITY_NAME is 'ITEM_REVISION_ENTITY then item revision Attachments.
