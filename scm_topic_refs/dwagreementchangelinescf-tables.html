DW_AGREEMENT_CHANGE_LINES_CF

Contains details for Purchase Agreement Lines for Accepted Agreement Versions. Data for Cancelled Purchase Agreements and Cancelled Change Order Versions will not be available. Also, data will appear only when some changes happen for the Purchase Agreement lines as part of the Agreement Version (Changes may happen for details which are not available on the Fact)

Details

Module: Purchasing
