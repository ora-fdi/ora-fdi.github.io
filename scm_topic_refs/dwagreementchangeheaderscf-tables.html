DW_AGREEMENT_CHANGE_HEADERS_CF

Contains details for Purchase Agreement Headers for Accepted Agreement Versions. Data for Cancelled Purchase Agreements and Cancelled Change Order Versions will not be available. Also, data will appear only when some changes happen for the Purchase Agreement headers as part of the Agreement Version (Changes may happen for details which are not available on the Fact)
